Privacy Policy for SSEC Pass Portal

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SSEC Pass Portal ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and student ID
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Department and course information
  • Emergency contact details
  • Pass request information (outing dates, reasons, destinations)

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • App usage statistics
  • Login timestamps
  • Feature usage patterns

2.3 Location Information

  • We may collect location data when you submit pass requests
  • Location data is used only for pass verification purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Pass Management: Processing and managing student pass requests
  • Authentication: Verifying user identity and access permissions
  • Communication: Sending notifications about pass status updates
  • Analytics: Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and institutional requirements
  • Security: Preventing fraud and ensuring app security

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Institutional Sharing

Your information may be shared with:

  • Department Heads (HODs) for pass approval
  • Wardens and security personnel for verification
  • Administrative staff for record-keeping
  • Emergency contacts when necessary

4.2 Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Cloud storage and hosting
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Push notifications
  • Payment processing (if applicable)

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and permissions
  • Data backup and recovery procedures

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Student pass records are typically retained for the duration of your enrollment plus 2 years.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for college students and educational staff. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Sending email notifications
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
